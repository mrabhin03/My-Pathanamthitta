Reasons to Visit Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is where nature’s calmness blends beautifully with spirituality, offering visitors a sense of peace that’s truly unique. The district is renowned for its rich traditions, from the world-famous Aranmula Kannadi (metal mirror) to the intricate Pathanamthitta mural paintings. Together, these cultural treasures and serene landscapes create an experience that stays with you forever.

Top Destinations

Aranmula

Aranmula is a heritage village in Pathanamthitta, Kerala, famous for its ancient Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple, the unique Aranmula Kannadi (metal mirror), and the vibrant Vallamkali (snake boat race) held during Onam. Known for its rich culture, art, and traditions, Aranmula is a spiritual and cultural hub that attracts visitors from around the world.
